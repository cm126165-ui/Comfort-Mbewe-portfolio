01. ABOUT ME

Practical software for regulated businesses and serious operators.

I'm a software developer based in Lusaka, Zambia, focused on building practical, high-impact web applications for businesses in Zambia's financial and commercial sectors. I build tools that solve real problems — not just demos.

My flagship product, LoanTrack, is a full-featured loan management and regulatory compliance system built specifically for Zambia's lending industry. It was designed in direct response to the Banking and Financial Services Act No. 9 of 2026, giving small and medium lenders the systems they need to meet Bank of Zambia requirements.

I'm currently pursuing a BSc in Computing at Cavendish University Zambia while actively building and selling software commercially. I'm available for freelance development work, software licensing, and consulting.

04. FEATURED PRODUCT

LoanTrack is designed to turn compliance into operational confidence.

AVAILABLE FOR LICENSING

LoanTrack v4.0

The compliance management system for Zambia's regulated lenders

Built for lenders that need more than a spreadsheet and more discipline than a generic CRM can provide. LoanTrack helps teams manage borrower records, repayment operations, compliance evidence, and lender reporting from one focused operating system.

BoZ Compliance Ready

Automated monthly returns, KYC verification, audit trails, and document vault — everything the Bank of Zambia requires.

Complete Loan Lifecycle

From borrower onboarding to final repayment. Track every payment, generate loan agreements, and monitor your portfolio in real time.

Secure & Auditable

Role-based access control, auto-logout on inactivity, and a complete audit log of every action in the system.

Built for the BFSA No. 9 of 2026

Every requirement from Zambia's new financial services law is addressed:

KYC & Borrower Verification
AML/CFT Suspicious Transaction Flagging
Monthly BoZ Return Generation (PDF)
Loan Cost-of-Credit Disclosure Documents
Compliance Dashboard & Readiness Score
Operational & IT Audit Trail
Document Vault for Policy Manuals
Data Protection Act Consent Logging
Interest Rate Compliance Monitoring
Role-Based Staff Access Control
